About Us

Learn more about DoggoScope and our mission

DoggoScope is dedicated to providing the best experience for dog lovers everywhere. Our mission is to connect people with their furry friends through innovative technology and heartfelt stories.

Technology

Using advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms, DoggoScope can analyze images of dogs to provide information about:

  • Breed identification with high accuracy
  • Estimated age based on visual markers
  • Mood and emotional state analysis
  • Activity level and energy assessment
